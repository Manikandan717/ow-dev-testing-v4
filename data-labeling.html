What is Data Labeling?

Data Labeling is a process of adding informative labels to the raw data, such as images, text files, videos, and Lidar etc to create high-quality training datasets for your machine learning(ML) models.

Artificial intelligence (AI) is only as good as the data it is trained with. On Average, Data Scientist spend 80% of the time in Data Curation or Wrangling which includes Data Labeling. Objectways assist with all your Data Labeling initiatives.

Computer Vision

Computer vision is a field of artificial intelligence (AI) that enables computers and systems to derive meaningful information from digital images, videos and other visual inputs and take actions or make recommendations based on that information. If AI enables computers to think, computer vision enables them to see, observe and understand.

Natural Language Processing

Natural language processing (NLP) uses machine learning to reveal the structure and meaning of text. With natural language processing applications, organizations can analyze text and extract information about people, places, and events to better understand social media sentiment and customer conversations.
