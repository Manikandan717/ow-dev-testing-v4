Compliance and Certifications

Our clients count on us to keep their information - and their customers information safe. That's why we completed formal certifications that affirms our commitment to security, privacy control and Infrastructure.

SOC2 Type2

SOC2 Type II compliance provides a higher level of assurance than other types of SOC compliance. SOC2 Type II compliance requires an independent audit that assesses the organization's internal controls over the course of a minimum of six months.It is also widely recognized standard for data security and confirm to AICPA standard.

ISO 27001

The ISO 27001 standard requires companies to identify information security risks to their system and the corresponding controls to address thern. ISO 27001 comprises 114 controls divided into 14 categories. ISO 27001 is considered the global gold standard for ensuring the security of information and data. We participated in the audit to showcase what security means to Objectways.

ISO 27701:2019

ISO 27701 provides the ideal mechanism for managing compliance with regulation from multiple jurisdictions around the world. By complying with the requirements of ISO 27701, we generate documentary evidence on how we process personally identifiable information (PII).

TPN

Our organization has achieved certification as a Trusted Partner Network (TPN). This certification signifies our unwavering commitment to the highest standards of security and content protection in the entertainment industry. With TPN certification, we ensure that our clients can trust us as a reliable and secure partner for their content needs.

HIPAA

The Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) is a landmark piece of legislation in the United States healthcare industry. HIPAA is now more widely known in another context:the improvement of data privacy and data security in the healthcare industry. At Objectways, we take security very seriously and that is why all our associates goes through HIPAA training as soon as they join as.

GDPR

GDPR is important because it improves the protection of European data subjects'rights and clarifies what companies that process personal data must do to safeguard these rights.
