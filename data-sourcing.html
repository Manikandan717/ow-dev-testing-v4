What is Data Sourcing?

Data sourcing is the process by which companies collect variety of data to train the machine learning models. The key to success for any machine learning model is to train with different types of datasets. Less or inaccurate data will lead to bias predictions.

Also, in order for an AI model to be trained correctly, the data needs to be high-quality, diverse and ethically sourced. This is why sourcing a wide variety of data is important.

Crowd Sourcing

Crowd Sourcing involves obtaining work, information, or opinions from a large group of people who submit their data via the Internet, social media, and smartphone apps.
Crowd Sourcing work allows companies to save time and money while tapping into people with different skills or thoughts from all over the world.

Audio & Video Sourcing

Each human voice and speech pattern is unique. They differ in intonation, pace, pronunciation and dialect. These factors complicate the development of automated speech recognition systems.
