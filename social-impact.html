Empowering Women

Creating jobs for women is especially important for any community transformation. Research shows that women will spend up to 90% of their income on health, nutrition, and education for their families. At Objectways, we hired more than 50% women and committed to extend that percentage to $5% in the coming years. Turning women into breadwinners not only builds self-esteem, but improves their standing in the community and enables them to pool resources and improve infrastructure. We not only make the best labels for your AI models, but through training and sustainable job creation, we empower our annotators and their families to break the cycle of poverty and build a brighter future.
